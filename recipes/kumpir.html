Kumpir

Description

Turkish style baked potatoes - vegan, simple, delicious.

Ingredients

Kumpir

  • 1 large potato per person
  • Olive oil
  • 1 bedda Creamy Spread Bell Pepper
  • 1 bedda Grated Mozzarella Style
  • Salt, pepper

Topping as desired

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Corn
  • Kidney beans
  • Couscous salad
  • Soy yogurt with garlic
  • Bedda Greek Style
  • Red cabbage
  • Olives
  • Sauerkraut
  • Hot peppers
  • Green salads
  • Fresh herbs
  • Bedda Hollandaise

Steps

  1. Scrub the potatoes with a brush and then prick them with a fork. Rub each potato with a teaspoon of olive oil, some salt and pepper. Wrap in aluminum foil and place on a baking sheet. Bake at 220 Â°C for 60-90 minutes, depending on the size of the potatoes.
  2. Cut the potatoes in half and add one tablespoon each of bedda Creamy Spread Bell Pepper and bedda Mozzarella Style. Lightly mash the inside of the potatoes with a fork so that the cheese melts a little.
  3. Serve the kumpir promptly with your favorite toppings.
